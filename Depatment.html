Historical Background


After independence, there was the only college at Kolhapur i.e. The Rajaram College, (established in 1880), catering to the needs of higher education. Only the students from upper classes could reap the benefits of that facility. Consequently, students from weaker classes from Kagal were deprived of higher education, as there was no institution of higher learning in and around Kagal.

  • B.COM

    Brief Description Department of Commerce established in the year June 1984. Department of Commerce is one of the oldest and leading departments with UG and PG section in the college. With regular teaching of syllabus the department arranges different activities such as classroom seminars, group discussions, debates, various competitions, industrial visits , etc. These activities cater needs of the students from various angles such as skill development in management and entrepreneurship. The department develops skills among students to face challenges in global scenario.


  • M.COM

    rief Description The Commerce Department has been operating at the degree level since the establishment of the college. Nevertheless, the college does not have post-graduate education facilities for the students of Commerce Department like M.Com, MBA and M.S.W. Our students had no choice but to go to Kolhapur City for postgraduate courses like these. As a result, there is a high rate of discontinuation of girls' post-graduate education. In view of these matters, M.Com classes have been started in college since June 2016.
